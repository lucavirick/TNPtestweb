Cloud Migration

ARCHITECTURE MODERNIZATION AND PLATFORM REALIGNMENT

Legacy environments frequently accumulate structural complexity that constrains scalability and increases operational risk. Cloud migration programs are therefore executed with an emphasis on architectural rationalization, controlled transition, and long-term maintainability.

Complex application and data estates are assessed, streamlined, and migrated across major enterprise platforms. Redundant components are retired, integration patterns are standardized, and cloud-aligned operating models are established to support elasticity, security, and performance at scale.
